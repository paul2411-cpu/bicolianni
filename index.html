Where Italy Meets the Philippines

Experience the exciting collision of culinary worlds with BICOLIANNI. Our Story: A Fusion of Passion and Flavor

Discover the art of sauce perfection

Bicolianni was born from a love for two iconic cuisines the hearty tradition of Italian pasta and the fiery, coconut-rich depth of Bicol Express from the Philippines. Created by Marketing Management students who believe food can bring cultures together, Bicolianni invites you to taste something daring, delightful, and unforgettable.

What Makes Bicolianni Special?

Authentic Italian Pasta: Al dente and made with quality durum wheat.

Signature Bicol Express Sauce: Creamy, spicy, with a touch of coconut made from a cherished Filipino recipe.
