About Us

Welcome

BICOLIANNI – Where culinary traditions collide in the most delicious way. This bold fusion dish brings together the timeless elegance of classic Italian pasta with the fiery, flavorful punch of the iconic Filipino Bicol Express. Indulge in a rich, creamy coconut-based sauce infused with chili, shrimp paste, and savory spices, generously coating al dente Italian noodles. Each bite delivers a mouthwatering symphony of spice, creaminess, and umami—a true celebration of two cultures on one unforgettable plate.
